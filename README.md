## Business Objective

To analyze Restaurant Sales, Menu Performance, and Customer Demand Patterns using Power BI, enabling data-driven decisions for operational efficiency, pricing optimization, and demand-based planning.

The goal of this project is to enable:

* Operational Performance Monitoring for Restaurant Managers
* Menu Optimization Insights for Business Growth
* Demand-Based Staffing and Resource Planning
* Revenue and Pricing Intelligence for Strategic Decisions

---

## Project Brief

This Power BI-based Restaurant Analytics Report evaluates business performance across three core operational dimensions:

1. Operational Overview
2. Menu & Pricing Overview
3. Time & Demand Optimization

The report transforms transactional restaurant data into actionable insights, helping stakeholders identify:

* Revenue trends and demand patterns
* Menu performance and pricing effectiveness
* Peak and off-peak operational hours
* Opportunities for cost optimization and growth

## Key Insights

### 1️⃣ Operational Performance

* Revenue remains stable with moderate fluctuations across the period.
* Demand is evenly distributed across weekdays with slight peaks on Monday and Sunday.
* Evening snacks and lunch contribute the highest order volumes.

---

### 2️⃣ Menu & Pricing

* Revenue is well distributed with no over-reliance on a single item (Top Item Revenue = 6.63%).
* Italian cuisine has the highest average pricing, while American cuisine has the lowest.
* Several menu items show consistently low demand, indicating optimization opportunities.

---

### 3️⃣ Time & Demand Patterns

* Peak demand occurs at **12 PM**, aligning with lunch hours.
* Lowest demand occurs at **10 AM**, indicating low early-hour activity.
* Weekdays account for the majority of orders (~72%).
* Peak hour contributes approximately **12% of total orders**, indicating moderate demand concentration.

---

## Business Recommendations

### For Operations Management

* Align staffing with peak demand hours (12 PM – 6 PM).
* Reduce staffing during low-demand periods to optimize costs.

---

### For Menu Strategy

* Reassess low-performing menu items for removal or repositioning.
* Promote mid-performing items to improve revenue distribution.

---

### For Pricing Strategy

* Evaluate pricing alignment across categories to balance demand and profitability.
* Introduce value-based pricing for underperforming categories.

---

### For Business Growth

* Implement targeted promotions during off-peak hours.
* Expand high-performing cuisine categories to drive revenue growth.
